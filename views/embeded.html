Help in other languages

The multicultural Problem Gambling Service provides counselling in more than 40 community langauges. You can talk to them by phone, or arrange to meet with a counsellor who speaks your language.

Gambling help is offered in all languages, including Chinese, Vietnamese, Arabic & Italian.

Financial counselling

Financial counsellors are trained professionals who can help you to work your way out of money difficulties resulting from gambling.

There are many financial counsellors and organisations who can help you work your way out of money difficulties as a result of gambling.
